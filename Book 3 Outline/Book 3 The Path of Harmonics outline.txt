Book 3: The Path of Harmonics
Core Premise:
Lucien (Ethan) feels the pull toward the Twelfth Harmonic—23 truths about cosmic structure that will allow him to question the Five Divine Currents' authority. As he pursues this path with his loved ones, Tharion investigates Aeloran's millennia-long manipulation, revealing the guardian deity's dual motive: saving Dravaryn and engineering his own freedom. The journey transforms Lucien, forcing him to separate his stolen Earth-soul (23% fragment) to achieve perfect alignment. He reaches the Twelfth and dissolves into cosmic principle, restructuring reality to make consent fundamental—but at the cost of his humanity. The fragment, preserved in Mira's wisp, pulses with longing, setting up Book 4's retrieval mission.
